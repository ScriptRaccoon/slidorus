About this puzzle

Slidorus is a sliding puzzle made up of 9 blocks, each containing 9 pieces, arranged in a Sudoku-like 9×9 grid. Every row and column can be shifted in both directions; use your mouse on desktop or your fingers on a phone. When a piece moves out of the puzzle on one side, it reappears on the opposite side. In other words, the pieces move on a torus.

The puzzle is similar to 9×9 Loopover, but with a different color scheme and indistinguishable pieces within each 3×3 block. The puzzle is considered solved when each 3×3 block is uniformly colored; the exact positions of the blocks do not matter.
