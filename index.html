Torus Slider

About this puzzle

Torus Slider is a sliding puzzle consisting of 9 blocks of 9 pieces, arranged like in a Sudoku. Each row and each column can be moved in both directions. When a piece is about to move out of the puzzle, it comes back at the opposite side. Hence, the pieces actually move on a torus.

The puzzle is very similar to Loopover of size 9x9, but with a different color scheme and with indistuishable pieces within each 3x3 block. Moreover, the puzzle is already solved when each 3x3 block is single-color, i.e., the positions of the blocks do not matter.

Usage: Drag a row or column to move it. This works both on touch and desktop devices. On desktop devices, you can also hover over a piece and then move its row or column with the 4 arrow keys.
