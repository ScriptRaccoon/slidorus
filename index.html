Slidorus

About this puzzle

Slidorus is a sliding puzzle consisting of 9 blocks of 9 pieces, arranged like in a Sudoku. Each row and each column can be moved in both directions. Simply use your mouse on desktop or your fingers on the phone. When a piece is about to move out of the puzzle, it comes back at the opposite side. Hence, the pieces actually move on a torus.

The puzzle is very similar to Loopover of size 9x9, but with a different color scheme and with indistuishable pieces within each 3x3 block. Moreover, the puzzle is already solved when each 3x3 block is single-color, i.e., the positions of the blocks do not matter.
